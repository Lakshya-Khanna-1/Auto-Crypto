# 📈 Auto Crypto Trader: Complete Beginner's Guide

Welcome to the **Auto Crypto Trader**! This guide is designed to help you run, observe, and control your automated trading bot, even if you have absolutely no background in coding or finance.

---

## 🚀 1. The 1-Minute Quick Start

The trading platform is designed to be fully automated. You control it using simple double-click files located in the main folder:

*   **To inspect logs in real-time (Standard Mode):**
    Double-click **`run_platform.bat`**. This opens a black terminal window and shows exactly what the bot is doing second-by-second. Keep this window open to keep the bot running.
*   **To run in the background (Invisible Mode):**
    Double-click **`run_background.bat`**. This starts the bot invisibly. You can close all windows, work on your PC, or leave it running for weeks without any open terminal screens.
*   **To stop the bot completely:**
    Double-click **`stop_platform.bat`**. This instantly shuts down the background processes.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The bot requires an active internet connection and your PC to remain powered on to execute trades.

---

## 🖥️ 2. Accessing Your Dashboard

Once the bot is started, you can watch it trade, view live charts, and monitor open positions from your web browser.

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Brave, etc.).
2. Go to the following address:
   **[http://localhost:9090/dashboard/static/index.html](http://localhost:9090/dashboard/static/index.html)**
3. The premium dashboard will load.

---

## 📊 3. Exploring the Web Dashboard Panels

The dashboard is split into 6 interactive panels:

### 🟢 Panel 1: Main Control Panel
*   **Mode Switcher**: Displays whether you are in **PAPER** (simulated sandbox with no real money) or **LIVE** trading mode. 
*   **Active Equity & Balance**: Displays the starting balance ($10,000.00 default) and your current total net worth (cash + open positions value).

### 🛡️ Panel 2: Risk Control & Kill-Switch
If the market undergoes a severe crash or the bot experiences unexpected errors, the system triggers the **Kill-Switch** to lock operations and prevent losses.
*   If the dashboard displays a flashing red **"KILL SWITCH ACTIVE"** banner, the bot is locked.
*   **How to unlock (re-arm):** Click the **RE-ARM** button, type **`RE-ARM`** in the pop-up confirmation box, and press enter. The bot will automatically unlock and resume trading.

### 📜 Panel 3: Trade History Log
*   Shows a completed chronological ledger of all buy and sell orders the bot has executed, including timestamps, quantities, filled prices, and total commission fees.

### 📝 Panel 4: Live Activity Console
*   Shows the exact system log feed. It highlights when the bot checks the strategy indicators, synchronizes market candle history, or polls ticker prices.

### 🌐 Panel 5: Network Connections
*   Shows green status lights if the bot is successfully connected to the Binance data feed, the local processor feed, and the AI Layer engine.

### 🤖 Panel 6: Positions & AI Insights
*   **Positions Tab**: Displays active open trades. You can see the entry price, how much profit/loss the trade is currently carrying (Unrealized P&L), and the **Trailing Stop Price** (the automated safety price that moves up as the coin rises and sells to lock in profit if the coin falls).
*   **AI Report Tab**: Shows a daily summary generated by the AI model analyzing trade statistics, risk drawdowns, and strategy performance.

## 🔬 4. Testing & Simulating a Trade Instantly

Since the bot is currently configured to analyze 5-minute charts, trades naturally occur once every 1 to 2 hours. If you want to instantly verify that order routing, trailing stop protection, and database tracking are fully functional without waiting:

1. Open a command prompt or terminal in the project folder.
2. Run the following command:
   ```powershell
   .venv\Scripts\python.exe -c "import sys; sys.path.insert(0, '.'); import scripts.trigger_test_trade as t; import asyncio; asyncio.run(t.main())"
   ```
3. Open your browser dashboard. You will see a live trade execute immediately, followed by the automated stop-loss safety net closing the position when real market prices update.

---

## 🧹 5. Resetting Your Paper Sandbox

If you want to clear your trade history, wipe open positions, and restore your paper account balance back to the starting $10,000.00:

1. Double-click **`stop_platform.bat`** (or make sure the bot is running).
2. Open a terminal or running command prompt and run this command:
   ```powershell
   curl.exe -s -X POST http://localhost:9090/api/paper/reset
   ```
3. Your dashboard will refresh and start clean.

## 🔄 6. Continuous 30-Day Cycles & AI Retraining

The bot is governed by an automated supervisor script. Here is what happens over the long term:
*   The supervisor keeps the bot running for a **30-day cycle**.
*   At the end of 30 days, the supervisor automatically pauses trading, fetches all historical price action from the past month, **retrains the machine learning predictive models**, restarts the engine, and begins the next 30-day trading cycle with the capital made or lost.
*   This cycle repeats indefinitely in the background!
